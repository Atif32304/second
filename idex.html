Conditions and Etiquette of Azan

There are some conditions for Muezzin and some for Adhan, both are as follows:

Conditions for Muezzin:

(1) To be a Muslim, (2) To be unmarried, (3) To be wise.

Conditions of Adhan:

(1) To be in time, (2) To be in Arabic language, (3) To follow the sequence of words, (4) To be in succession i.e. not to talk in between.

Etiquette of Adhan:

(1) Loudness, (2) The muezzin should be honest and righteous, (3) Keeping your fingers in your ears at the time of Adhan, (4) The words of Adhan should be fixed while performing Iqamah with haste, (5) Qibla. Turning to the side, (6) Turning to the right in Haya Ali al-Salaat and to the left in Haya Ali Al Falah. ..
Also, the call to prayer of a woman and a child who does not understand is not correct; Therefore, if either of the two gave the call to prayer, then it will be necessary to repeat it, but if the child is old enough to understand, then the call to prayer will be correct, but it is better that a mature and sensible person calls the call to prayer. Only God knows.
